QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions

Why do some providers hide fees?

Sometimes promotional pricing focuses on headline monthly costs, burying details in small print.

Are providers required to disclose price rises?

Yes, Ofcom requires providers to clearly explain pricing policies.

What is a minimum guaranteed speed?

The lowest speed your provider guarantees during peak times — you may be able to leave if not met.

Should I always choose the most transparent provider?

Transparency helps you make informed decisions and avoid surprises.

Can comparison tools identify transparent deals?

Good comparison tools show full pricing details and contract terms.

What's the most common hidden cost?

Annual price rises — many providers increase prices mid-contract.
