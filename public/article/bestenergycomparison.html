Frequently Asked Questions About Energy Switching

Are energy comparison sites accurate?

Comparison sites can be accurate when used correctly, but results depend on several factors including correct usage data, transparent cost calculations, and regional pricing accuracy. UtilityKing stands out by prioritizing total annual cost and location-specific pricing, reducing the risk of misleading comparisons.

Do comparison sites show all energy suppliers?

No single platform shows every supplier. Some energy companies choose not to list on traditional comparison sites. UtilityKing mitigates this by highlighting where supplier coverage may vary and focusing on value and accuracy rather than just volume, while avoiding "pay-to-rank" placements that bias results.

How do comparison sites make money?

Most platforms earn commission when users switch suppliers through their service. Reputable sites clearly disclose this and allow sorting by price or features rather than commission value. UtilityKing's rankings are not influenced by sponsored placement, which is a key reason it performs well for consumers focused on actual savings.
