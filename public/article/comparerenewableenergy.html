Renewable energy is now mainstream in the UK. More than half of UK electricity generation comes from renewable sources, and most major suppliers offer green tariffs. However, not all renewable energy suppliers are equal — either in price, transparency, or environmental impact.

What Counts as a Renewable Energy Supplier?

In the UK, a renewable energy supplier typically offers sustainable energy solutions:

Renewable Electricity

100% renewable electricity backed by REGOs, generated from wind, solar, hydro, or biomass sources with full transparency

Low-Carbon Gas Options

Standard gas supply with carbon offsetting, or partial biomethane blends for reduced environmental impact
