THE BASICS

What is ping time?

⏱️ Ping measures the round-trip time for data: your device → server → back. Measured in milliseconds (ms). Lower is better.
PingPerformance Level
1–10msExcellent
10–20msVery good
20–40msGood
40–60msNoticeable delay
60ms+Lag likely

For gaming and real-time apps, lower and more stable ping is crucial.

Does higher speed mean lower ping?

No. 300 Mbps FTTP and 1Gbps FTTP have similar ping. Ping depends on infrastructure, routing, and distance — not speed.
