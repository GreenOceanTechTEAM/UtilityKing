When Contact Details Are Required (Important)

While comparison can be done anonymously, contact details are required if you request a tailored quote or choose to switch supplier.

This is because energy suppliers must:

  • Verify property and meter details
  • Confirm eligibility for specific tariffs
  • Generate accurate, personalised pricing
  • Contact you if clarification is needed
  • Set up an account if you proceed

Key Points:

👉 Simply viewing prices does not require contact details.

👉 Requesting a personalised supplier quote does require contact details.
