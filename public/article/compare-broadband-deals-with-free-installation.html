What does “free installation” actually mean?

Covers activation fee, standard engineer visit, line setup. May not include router delivery fees, premium installation, or non‑standard wiring. Always check the small print.

When is installation usually free?

Most common on 18‑month or 24‑month contracts, promotional full fibre deals, and new customer offers. Shorter contracts often include setup fees.

Fibre vs full fibre installation costs
Standard (FTTC)

Remote activation often free – no engineer required in most cases.

Full fibre (FTTP)
