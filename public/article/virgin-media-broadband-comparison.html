HOW IT WORKS

Virgin Media operates on its own cable network (separate from Openreach). It uses fibre to cabinets and coaxial cable to homes (DOCSIS), with ongoing FTTP upgrades. Availability is postcode-specific, speeds can be very high, but infrastructure varies by area.

💰 Pricing & contracts

Virgin typically offers 18‑month contracts, promotional new‑customer pricing, CPI‑linked annual rises (CPI +3.9% common). Always check setup fees, installation, post‑promotion price, early exit fees.

📺 TV & bundle options

Virgin is known for strong bundles: broadband + TV + sports + cinema + landline. Bundling simplifies but increases monthly cost. If you don't need premium TV, broadband‑only may be cheaper.

24‑MONTH COST EXAMPLE

❓ Frequently asked questions

Is Virgin faster than Sky/BT?

Often yes for download – but upload may be lower than full fibre.

Is Virgin gigabit worth it?

For heavy downloaders, yes. Most households fine with 250–350 Mbps.

Does Virgin use fibre?

Partially – fibre to cabinet + coax to home (ongoing FTTP upgrades).

Is Virgin reliable?

Generally yes, but performance varies by area.
