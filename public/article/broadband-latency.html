THE BASICS

What is latency (ping)?

⏱️ Latency is the time data travels from your device → game server → and back. Measured in milliseconds (ms). Lower is better.
5ms = Excellent
15ms = Great
30ms = Good
50ms+ = Noticeable
100ms+ = Lag

In competitive games (FPS, battle royale), low and stable latency is critical.

Does Gigabit improve ping?

No. Gigabit improves download speed and update times, but does not significantly reduce latency compared to 300 Mbps FTTP.
