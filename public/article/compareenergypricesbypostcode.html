📍 Why Energy Prices Vary by Postcode

Energy prices in the UK are not the same everywhere. Even if two households use the same amount of gas and electricity, their bills can differ significantly depending on postcode.

Key reasons for price differences:
  • The UK energy grid is split into regional distribution zones
  • Each region has different network costs
  • Standing charges and unit rates vary by location
  • Suppliers price tariffs differently by region

👉 To find the cheapest energy deal, you must compare prices by postcode.
