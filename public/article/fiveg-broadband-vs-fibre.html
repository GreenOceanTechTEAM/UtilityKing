THE BIG QUESTION

Can 5G replace fibre?

In strong 5G areas: Yes for light-to-moderate users

For heavy households and remote workers: Fibre remains more reliable

Temporary solution Backup connection Rural alternative

⚡ 5G vs Gigabit Fibre

Gigabit fibre (900–1,000 Mbps): faster than typical 5G, lower latency, more stable. If available at competitive pricing, it outperforms 5G.
