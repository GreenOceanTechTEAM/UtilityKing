QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions

How often should I compare broadband?

At least once per year, or before your contract ends.

When is the best time to switch broadband?

Around 30-60 days before your contract expires.

Can I switch broadband mid-contract?

Yes, but exit fees may apply — often better to wait until contract ends.

Will switching interrupt my internet?

Downtime is usually minimal — providers coordinate the transfer.

Do broadband deals change often?

Yes, providers frequently introduce new promotions and offers.

What's the biggest savings opportunity?

Switching from out-of-contract standard rates to new customer deals.
