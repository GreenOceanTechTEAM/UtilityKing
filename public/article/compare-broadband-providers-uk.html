Frequently Asked Questions

Q Which broadband provider is cheapest?

Varies by postcode and promotions. Entry-level fibre starts from £22–£25/month.

Q Which offers the fastest speeds?

Full fibre providers offer up to 1Gbps. Availability depends on your area.

Q Do all use the same network?

Most use Openreach, but Virgin Media and alt-nets operate separate infrastructure.

Q Can I switch under contract?

Yes, but early termination fees may apply.

Q Is full fibre worth it?

If available and competitively priced, full fibre offers better reliability and future-proof speeds.
