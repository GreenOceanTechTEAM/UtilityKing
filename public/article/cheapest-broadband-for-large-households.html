Frequently Asked Questions

Q Is 150 Mbps enough for 5 people?

Yes, for moderate use. Heavy 4K streaming households may prefer 300 Mbps.

Q Do large households need gigabit broadband?

Not usually — unless usage is extreme.

Q Is full fibre necessary?

Strongly recommended for stability and upload performance.

Q How can I reduce broadband costs?

Switch when out of contract and compare deals regularly.

Q How often should we review broadband?

At least once per year.
