📈 What is Decentralized Energy Generation?

Decentralized energy means generating power directly on-site, using technologies like solar panels, wind turbines, or combined heat and power (CHP) systems. This approach gives businesses autonomy, resilience, and opportunities to feed excess electricity back into the grid for additional revenue.

📢 Fact: By 2030, nearly 50% of UK businesses are expected to adopt on-site energy generation.

⚡ Top Benefits of On-Site Energy Production

  1. Major Cost Savings: Cut bills by 30–60% and protect against volatile grid prices.
  2. Energy Independence: Enhance resilience against grid disruptions.
  3. Net Zero Goals: Reduce carbon emissions by up to 80%.
  4. New Revenue Streams: Sell surplus energy or store it for peak-hour use.
📢 Fact: Most businesses achieve full ROI within 5–7 years, with >12% annual returns.

🔄 Key Considerations Before Investing

  • Energy Usage: Assess your energy demand profile to size the system correctly.
  • Space & Infrastructure: Review available roof or land area for installations.
  • Initial Investment: Plan financing and evaluate ROI timelines.
  • Grid Connection: Understand export options through SEG and backup mechanisms.
📢 Fact: Solar + battery setups can boost energy self-sufficiency by up to 70%.

📊 How Battery Storage Enhances On-Site Power

  • Store surplus solar or wind energy for later use.
  • Reduce dependency on grid energy during high-cost periods.
  • Maintain operations seamlessly during power interruptions.
📢 Fact: Battery integration reduces grid reliance by 60–80%.

💰 Government Incentives & Grants

Businesses can access several UK government programs to offset initial investment costs:

  • Smart Export Guarantee (SEG) – Earn by exporting surplus electricity to the grid.
  • Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI) – Support for renewable heating systems.
  • Low Carbon Grants – Assistance for solar, wind, and storage solutions.
  • Capital Allowances – Tax relief for renewable energy installations.
📢 Fact: Incentives can reduce project costs by up to 50%.
